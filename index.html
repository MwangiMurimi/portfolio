About me

A young, budding programmer, I was born in 2000 in Nairobi, Kenya. I am a hardworking, leadership-minded, versatile African and aside from speaking three languages (English, Kiswahili and French), I have a firm understanding of HTML, CSS, Javascript and an array of soft skills under my arm such as problem solving skills, interpersonal skills, leadership skills and a strong work ethic.

My greatest attribute is my willingness to learn; after all, the biggest room in the world is room for improvement.

I am currently a student at Moringa School.
